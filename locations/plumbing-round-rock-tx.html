How does hard water in Round Rock affect my plumbing?
Round Rock, like much of Central Texas, has notoriously hard water with high concentrations of calcium and magnesium. Over time, these minerals form scale buildup inside your pipes, water heater, and appliances, reducing water flow and energy efficiency. This can lead to premature failure of fixtures and water heaters. QuickFix Plumbing recommends annual water heater flushing, scale-resistant fixtures, and professional water softener installation to protect your plumbing system from hard water damage.

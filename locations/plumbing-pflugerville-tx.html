What are the signs that I have a slab leak in my Pflugerville home?
Slab leaks occur when water lines running beneath your home's concrete foundation develop a leak. Common warning signs include unexplained increases in your water bill, the sound of running water when all fixtures are off, warm or damp spots on your floors, reduced water pressure, and cracks forming in your flooring or walls. Pflugerville's expansive clay soils can shift with seasonal moisture changes, which can stress underground pipes over time. If you notice any of these symptoms, call QuickFix Plumbing at 512-555-0199 immediately — early detection can prevent major structural damage.
